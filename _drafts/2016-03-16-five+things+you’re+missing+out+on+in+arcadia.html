There is an area in Phoenix I think you should know about called Arcadia. It’s a great area for five reasons:
  1. Location: It’s just about 10 minutes from the Sky Harbor Airport. If you travel a lot, this location makes it easy for you to come and go easily. The shopping is fantastic as well. The Biltmore Outdoor Fashion Park is absolutely gorgeous and the Scottsdale Fashion Square isn’t far either! 

  2. Food: There’s not enough time to cover all of the food options, so I’ll just share a few of them. La Grande Orange is a really funky and fun grocery and pizzeria; Chelsea’s Kitchen focuses on fresh Arizona cuisine and sits right on the canal; O.H.S.O Brewery is really fun because they have an outdoor patio where you can enjoy beautiful views of the mountains; and one of my favorite places is North Italia, which focuses on Northern Italian cuisine and is simply delicious

  3. Entertainment: Arcadia is literally just 10 minutes away from Old Town Scottsdale, which is great if you’re looking for a great place to eat or for some nightlife. Also, the Suns and the Diamondbacks play just 15 minutes away!  

  4. Outdoor activities: In Arcadia, you are only five minutes away from Camelback Mountain and Piestewa Peak, which are great for biking, walking, jogging, or any other outdoor activity you like to do!

  5. Real estate: There is a wide variety of real estate here. You can find single-family homes that range anywhere from $250,000 to $6 million! Lafayette and Exeter streets are beautiful and filled with custom homes. It’s a beautiful area I would love to show you some time!
